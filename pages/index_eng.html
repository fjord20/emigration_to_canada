About

Immigrant.Today – an information portal that helps people immigrate and adapt (settle down) in Canada

The project started in 2015. It began with a selection of YouTube channels on the topic of emigration and their transcriptions (text versions of videos). Over time, the site began to publish exclusive materials on how to get a visa to Canada, which program makes it easier to emigrate, how to find a first job in a new country, and much more.
